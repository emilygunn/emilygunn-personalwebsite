Home page

The Main/Home page will contain information "About Me." It will express my strengths such as being able to stay organized, and my willingness to listen and to learn. It will also contain my hobbies and things that I like to do such as ballet, art, and games. It will also have a description on my personality like how I am quiet and an introvert, but I am willing to work with and get to know people.

The skills page will include what I know related to Software Engineering.
